Recipe for Lasagna

  • Time: 1 hour 15 minutes
  • Serves: 8 servings
Ingredients Quantity
ground beef 450g
small onion 100g
minced garlic 2 cloves
marinara sause 650g (24oz)
dried basil 1 teaspoon
dried oregano 1 teaspoon
salt and pepper as you like
ricotta cheese 425g
mozzarella cheese 1.5 cup
parmesan cheese 1.5cup
egg 1
fresh parsley 1 teaspoon
lasagna noodles 9-12

Instrutions

Step 1: Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).

Step 2:Cook noodles: Boil the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.

Step 3: Prepare meat sauce: In a large skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Add chopped onion and minced garlic and sauté until soft. Stir in marinara sauce, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes, then remove from heat.

Step 4: Prepare cheese mixture: In a bowl, mix together the ricotta cheese, 1 cup mozzarella, Parmesan, egg, and a pinch of salt and pepper.

Step 5:Assemble lasagna: In a 9x13 inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom. Layer 4 noodles on top of the sauce. Spread 1/3 of the ricotta mixture over the noodles, then top with 1/3 of the meat sauce. Repeat the layering process two more times, ending with meat sauce on top.

Step 6: Top with mozzarella: Sprinkle the remaining mozzarella cheese on top.

Step 7: Bake: Cover the dish with foil (to prevent sticking, either spray foil with cooking spray or use non-stick foil). Bake for 25 minutes. Then, uncover and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until bubbly and golden.

Last Step: Rest and serve: Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before slicing. Serve hot and enjoy!
